S0NGLY: A Song Rating Platform

What is S0NGLY?

S0NGLY is an upcoming cross-platform music rating platform, designed to allow users to upload, rate, and review their favorite songs.

How to use S0NGLY?

Upload newly released or unlisted songs
Rate on-site songs on a 1 to 10 star scale
Write detailed reviews of songs and albums
Discuss reviews and ratings with fellow members
Follow your friends and update your profile
Create playlists of your favorite songs and genres
