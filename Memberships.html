S0NGLY Memberships

Three Price Tiers: Free, Monthly, Premium

  • Free Membership
  • Monthly Membership
  • Premium Membership

Free Membership

Price: Free
  • Users with a Free Membership will be able to rate and review five songs per hour.
  • Free Membership includes 30 second previews per song with occaisional ads.
  • Free Members have access to followers and following, and an about me section.
  • Return to Memberships or Return to Home

Monthly Membership

Price: $9.99/mo
  • Users with a Monthly Membership will be able to rate and review unlimited songs.
  • Monthly Members includes unlimited listening to songs with occaisional ads.
  • Monthly Members are able to make playlists with a limit of 100 songs per playlist.
  • Monthly Members will be able to customize their profile picture, as well as a badge denoting their membership visible to everyone.
  • Return to Memberships or Return to Home

Premium Membership

Price: $19.99/mo
  • Users with a Premium Membership will be able to rate and review unlimited songs.
  • Premium Members includes unlimited listening to songs with no ads.
  • Premium Members are able to make playlists with unlimited songs per playlist.
  • Premium Members will be able to customize their profile picture and banner, as well as a badge denoting their membership visible to everyone.
  • Premium Members will be able to upload GIFs as their profile picture and banner.
  • Premium Members will have their reviews, playlists, and profiles prioritized to the top of any listings.
  • Return to Memberships or Return to Home
